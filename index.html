Why Join?

Fix 80% of household plumbing issues on your own, stop waiting days for plumbers, and save RM100–RM300 per visit. Train with a certified plumber (30+ years’ experience) on real taps, traps, and toilet rigs. Tools and materials are provided.

Format: Theory + Live Demonstration + Hands-On Rotation Stations

What You’ll Learn

  • Locate and shut main/isolation valves safely
  • Replace tap washers & cartridges (washer & mixer types)
  • Install or replace flexible hoses and apply PTFE tape correctly
  • Clean sink P-traps & floor traps without harsh chemicals
  • Unclog floor traps using safe mechanical methods
  • Troubleshoot toilet flush issues (flapper/flush valve)
  • Build a personal starter plumbing toolkit
